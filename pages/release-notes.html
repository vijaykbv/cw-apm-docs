Known Issues

Current Limitations

  • Cross-account service maps may show incomplete data
  • RUM data collection limited to HTTPS sites
  • Synthetic canaries may timeout on complex scripts
  • High-cardinality metrics may impact performance

Workarounds

  • Use service-specific queries for cross-account scenarios
  • Implement custom RUM collection for HTTP sites
  • Break complex canaries into smaller, focused tests
  • Implement metric aggregation to reduce cardinality
